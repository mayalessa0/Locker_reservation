Locker Reservation System

This system aims to add a property in the electronic portal services on the official website of Princess Noura Bint AbdulRahman University to book lockers electronically. This feature will serve students and employees, as student can book a locker online and save a lot of time and effort, and employees official duties will be more organized and easy . An online system that supports English language, simple, powerful and effective to make reservations between students on the one hand and employees on the other without the need to follow the traditional steps in booking lockers. In the initial phase of the project, students from the College of Computer and Information Science will be targeted, and then applied to all colleges in the future.
